Architecture

How StealthPerp
protects traders.

Traditional perp DEXs write positions on-chain in plaintext — exposing every trader to copy-trading bots, front-runners, and targeted liquidation hunters. StealthPerp eliminates these attack vectors entirely.

01
📡

Encrypted Order Submission

When you submit an order, your size, direction, and price are encrypted client-side using Arcium's threshold encryption before they ever touch the network. The plaintext never leaves your browser.

02
⚡

MPC Matching & Liquidation

Arcium's Multiparty Computation clusters match orders and compute liquidation thresholds over ciphertexts. No single node — not even Arcium — can reconstruct your position. Checks happen privately.

03
🔓

PnL-Only Settlement

When your position closes, only the net PnL value is decrypted and settled on-chain to Solana. The original size, entry price, and liquidation level are never exposed — not even retroactively.

04
🌊

Deeper Liquidity

Because large traders are no longer exposed to front-running or targeted liquidations, they deploy bigger positions with confidence. Privacy directly enables deeper, more efficient markets for everyone.

Arcium Integration

Confidential
computing for
DeFi primitives

Arcium is a parallelized confidential computing network built on Solana. It enables arbitrary computation over encrypted data — meaning smart contracts can perform logic without ever seeing the inputs.

StealthPerp uses Arcium's MXE (Multiparty Execution) environment to run the three most privacy-critical operations: order matching, margin calculation, and liquidation threshold checking — all over sealed ciphertexts.

The result is a DEX where the protocol is mathematically prevented from leaking trader intent — not just promised not to.

Privacy Flow — Trade Lifecycle
U
User submits encrypted order
Position size, direction & price are Arcium threshold-encrypted in the browser. Ciphertext posted to Solana program.
A
Arcium MPC cluster processes order
3-of-5 MPC nodes jointly compute order matching, margin check, and liquidation price — operating entirely on ciphertexts. Zero plaintext exposure.
S
Position opened on Solana
An encrypted position commitment is stored on-chain. Mark price & oracle data are public; all sensitive fields remain sealed.
A
Liquidation checked privately
Arcium continuously evaluates encrypted margin ratios against oracle prices. Liquidation fires without ever revealing the threshold price publicly.
U
Only PnL revealed at settlement
On close, Arcium decrypts only the net profit/loss and settles USDC on Solana. Entry price, position size & liq price remain encrypted forever.
